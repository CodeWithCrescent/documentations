Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2025

Thank you for using TZ Voucher Recharge ("the App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • We do not require user accounts or registration
  • We do not track your usage patterns or behavior

2. Camera Usage

The App requests camera permission solely for the purpose of scanning voucher numbers. All image processing occurs locally on your device:

  • Camera images are processed in real-time and never stored
  • No images or scanned data are uploaded to any servers
  • You can revoke camera access anytime in your device settings

3. Voucher Data

When you scan or enter a voucher number:

  • The voucher number is used only to generate the USSD code
  • Voucher data remains on your device and is not collected by us
  • We do not store any transaction history

4. USSD Processing

The App initiates USSD codes through your mobile network provider:

  • We have no control over or access to USSD transaction details
  • All recharge transactions are between you and your mobile carrier

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses these third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google ML Kit: For on-device text recognition (OCR)
  • CameraX: For camera functionality

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
